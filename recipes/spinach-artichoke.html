Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip



Description

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheeses
  • Garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Artichoke hearts
  • Spinach

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a small baking dish.

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, basil, garlic salt, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl. Gently stir in artichoke hearts and spinach.

  3. Step 3

    4. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish; top with mozzarella cheese. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned, about 25 minutes.
